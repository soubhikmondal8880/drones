Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a drone for my event or project?

You can book a drone by selecting your preferred service (e.g., aerial photography, surveying, inspection), choosing a date and location, and completing the online booking form. Once submitted, a confirmation and further details will be sent to your email.

2. What types of drones are available for booking?

We offer a range of professional drones for photography, videography, mapping and inspections.

3. Is a drone operator included with the booking?

Yes, a certified drone operator is included with every booking.
